Let \(A=[a_{ij}]_{m\times n}\) and \(B=[b_{ij}]_{n\times r}\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} (AB)_{ij}=\sum_{k=1}^na_{ik}b_{kj}=\boldr_i\cdot \boldc_j\text{,} \end{equation*}
