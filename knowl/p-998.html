Let \(V\) be a vector space.

  1. For all \(\boldv\in V\text{,}\) we have \(0\boldv=\boldzero\text{.}\)

  2. For all \(c\in \R\text{,}\) we have \(c\boldzero=\boldzero\text{.}\)

  3. For all \(\boldv\in V\text{,}\) we have \((-1)\boldv=-\boldv\text{.}\)

  4. For all \(\boldv\in V\text{,}\) if \(c\boldv=\boldzero\text{,}\) then \(c=0\) or \(\boldv=\boldzero\text{.}\)
