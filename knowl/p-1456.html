Let \(A\) be an \(n\times n\) matrix.
  1. If \(A\) has a zero row or zero column, then \(\det A=0\)
  2. Assume \(n\geq 2\text{.}\) Let \(A'\) be the matrix obtained by swapping two rows (or two columns) of \(A\text{.}\) Then
    \begin{equation*} \det A'=-\det A\text{.} \end{equation*}
  3. Assume \(n\geq 2\text{.}\) If \(A\) has two identical rows or two identical columns, then \(\det A=0\text{.}\)
