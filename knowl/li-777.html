Item 2.

Clearly, if \(\boldv=\boldw\text{,}\) then \([\boldv]_B=[\boldw]_B\text{.}\) If \(T(\boldv)=T(\boldw)=(c_1,c_2,\dots, c_n)\text{,}\) then by definition of \([\phantom{\boldv}]_B\) we must have
\begin{equation*} \boldv=c_1\boldv_1+c_2\boldv_2+\cdots +c_n\boldv_n==\boldw\text{.} \end{equation*}
