We must show \(f(cA+dB)=cf(A)+df(B)\) for all scalars \(c,d\in\R\) and all matrices \(A, B\in M_{mn}\text{.}\) This follows easily from properties of transpose:

\begin{align*} f(cA+dB)\amp =(cA+dB)^T \amp \text{ (by def.) }\\ \amp =(cA)^T+(dB)^T \amp (\knowl{./knowl/th_trans_props.html}{\text{Theorem 3.2.11}})\\ \amp =cA^T+dB^T \amp (\knowl{./knowl/th_trans_props.html}{\text{Theorem 3.2.11}})\\ \amp =cf(A)+df(B) \end{align*}
