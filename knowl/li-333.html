Item 1.

Let \(T\colon V\rightarrow W\text{.}\) It is useful to keep in mind where \(\NS T\) and \(\im T\) “live” in this picture: we have \(\NS T\subseteq V\) and \(\im T\subseteq W\text{.}\) In other words, the null space is a subset of the domain, and the image is a subset of the codomain.
