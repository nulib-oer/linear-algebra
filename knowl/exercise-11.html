Exercise 6.

Suppose \({ A_i \ | \ i\in I }\) is an indexed family of sets. For each statement below, write the letter of the equivalent symbolic form.
  1. \(B\cap A_i \not = \emptyset\) for every \(i\)
  2. \(\displaystyle B \subseteq (\bigcup_{i \in I} A_i)\)
  3. \(B\subseteq A_i\) for some \(i\)
  1. \(\displaystyle (\forall i \in I) (\exists x \in B)(x \in A_i)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle (\exists i \in I)(\forall x \in B)(x \in A_i)\)
  3. \(\displaystyle (\forall x \in B)(\exists i \in I)(x \in A_i)\)
