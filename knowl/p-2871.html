  1. \(V=\R^4\) with dot product, \(\boldv=(1,-1,2,1)\)
  2. \(V=\R^3\) with dot product with weights \(1,2,3\text{,}\) \(\boldv=(3,1,-2)\)
  3. \(V=C([0,1])\) with integral inner product, \(\boldv=e^x\)
