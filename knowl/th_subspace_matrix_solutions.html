Theorem 3.3.10. Solutions to \(A\boldx=\boldzero\) form a subspace.

Let \(A\) be an \(m\times n\) matrix. The set
\begin{equation*} W=\{\boldx\in \R^n\colon A\boldx=\boldzero\} \end{equation*}
of solutions to the homogeneous matrix equation \(A\boldx=\boldzero\) is a subspace of \(\R^n\text{.}\)
